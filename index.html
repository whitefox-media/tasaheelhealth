About Us

Empowering Lives Through Compassionate Disability Care

Tasaheel Disability Care is a leading provider of specialized disability care and personalized home support services in Kuwait. We are dedicated to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities by offering compassionate, professional care that prioritizes safety, dignity, and comfort. Our team of trained caregivers works closely with each client and their families to deliver customized support tailored to their unique needs. At Tasaheel, we believe that every person deserves to live with respect, independence, and the highest quality of life—right in the comfort of their own home.

Our Services

Tracheostomy Care

We provide specialized tracheostomy care to ensure the safety, comfort, and respiratory health of individuals with a tracheostomy, including routine cleaning, monitoring, and emergency support.

Gastrostomy Care

We provide dedicated post-gastrectomy care to support recovery and long-term health, including wound care, nutritional management, and monitoring for complications following partial or full stomach removal.

Colostomy Care

We deliver compassionate and hygienic colostomy care, supporting individuals with stoma management, skin protection, and daily living adjustments to promote independence and wellbeing

Medication Administration

Our qualified professionals ensure safe and accurate medication administration, tailored to each client’s care plan, to support effective treatment and health outcomes.

Neurological Condition Care

We provide comprehensive care for individuals living with neurological conditions, offering physical, emotional, and daily living support designed to enhance quality of life and promote independence.

Nutrition Counseling

Our nutrition counseling services are personalized to meet individual health needs, offering guidance on diet plans, meal choices, and nutritional support to improve overall wellbeing.
